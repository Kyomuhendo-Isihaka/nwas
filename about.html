About Us

We Provide Best Tour Packages In Your Budget

Nature Whispers African Safaris is an East African tour and travel company that organizes trips and tours including bookings for accommodation. We are dedicated to ensuring a high level of professionalism and excellence in our services, so that our dear clients are delighted.

Our tour types includes custom and tailor made tours, within the East African region. Our destinations include Uganda, where we have our head offices, Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda and Congo. We also do exclusive high end safaris and group tours.

Some of our tour products and services include; Gorilla tracking, chimpanzee tracking, game drives , nature walks, rock climbing, mountain climbing, hiking, primate viewing, birding, community visits, car hiring, accommodation bookings and among others. We also have special packages for both our domestic and international tourists.

Slogan, “Tour beyond Nature”

Vision, “Our Vision is to serve our dear clients with a lifetime memorable safari experience with a high degree of professionalism in our operations so as to satisfy our clients”"

Mission: “To become the leading and most preferred tour and travel company within Africa, and marketing the eastern African region particularly Uganda, the pearl of Africa as the most preferred tourist destination in the whole world”

Core values: Time management, Trust Worthy, Integrity, Team work, Accountability, Value centricity, Customer care, and Respect
